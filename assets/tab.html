This is the first tableau sheet. It shows us the total sum of profits for the company within each of the 50 states within the United States of America. By hovering over the chart you can find specific profits for each specific state.

This is the second tableau sheet. It shows us the sum of the company's profits in California, by month for the last two years. By hovering over the chart, you can see the sum of the profits in California for a specific month of a specific year.

This is the third tableau sheet.It shows us how the sum of the profits of each category of goods within California.

This is the fourth tableau sheet. It shows the sum of the profits overtime for the office supply category, which is the company's most profitable category of goods. It also makes a forecast of how much profit the office supply category will generate through November 2015.
